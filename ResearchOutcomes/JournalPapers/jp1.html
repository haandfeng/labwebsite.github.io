Abstract

The integration of narrative design and information design is one of the hot spots in the field of design in recent years. While narrative provides a more humane perspective for digital information design, the explosion of information in the virtual world is causing users to lose the freshness of information. In this context, the interaction of entities as a bridge between the physical and virtual worlds has received a lot of attention. This paper proposes an information design framework from the perspective of entity interactive narrative, and designs TangibleTale, a children's narrative interaction tool.

Key words

Narrative Design, Physical Interaction, Human-Computer Interaction
