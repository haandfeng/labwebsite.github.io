Opportunities for Collaborations

In order to explore the future development of technology and better cooperate with enterprises. To develop high-tech products that meet the needs of the country and enterprises for development, our team has established close cooperative relationships with enterprises. Ways of cooperation include:

Become our partner;

We will conduct horizontal cooperation with enterprises, including providing technical services to enterprises in a certain direction or topic;

Establish joint laboratories (research centers) with enterprises and accept targeted donations from enterprises through the South China University of Technology Education Foundation.
